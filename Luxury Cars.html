Twenty-five years ago, luxury cars occupied the upper echelon of the automotive universe, the preferred transportation for individuals who had achieved a certain level of success. These days enormous SUVs have stolen much of that honor, but few of those behemoths can match the grace, understated elegance and distinguished pedigree of a traditional luxury sedan. Thanks to decades of engineering progress, luxury vehicles are more refined than ever, and many of them can cut and run with moves approaching sports-car levels of performance
